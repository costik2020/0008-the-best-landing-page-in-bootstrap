The #1 motorbike shop in London. Motorbike & Scooter dealer, repair, MOT & servicing

Welcome to MOTO STAR* London, We offer great value motorcycle repairs and servicing. We also do Motorcycle, Scooter and Moped roadside repairs, recovery and transport. If you need your bike moved or collected for work. Parts and accessories for all major brands of motorcycle.

  • Oil changes and Services.
  • Batteries, Brakes, Tyres and many parts in stock!.
  • Solving running and electrical issues.
  • Charging Issues and Starting problems.
  • Crash Repair and Plastic Repairs.
  • Metal Welding both Tig and Mig (Aluminium and Steel).

We are a small startup motorcycle and scooter repair shop located in London. We have many years experience working on any model or make of motorcycle and scooter. Bring us your dream bike and let us get you sorted.

We are specialists

Here at MOTOSTAR* we specialise in the repairs, servicing and MOT Testing of all makes & models of motorcycles inc. Aprilia, Benelli, Bmw, Buell, Ducati, Harley Davidson, Honda, Kawasaki, Ktm, Suzuki, Triumph and Yamaha.

We love Motorcycles

We just love bikes and have the special tools + equipment needed to compliment the skills + experience. We can do those little jobs as well as the bigger job's which other workshops can't or won't do, providing a personal one to one service is our main aim.

We do small jobs or big jobs

No job is to big or small, from a blown fuse to a total restoration. Providing you with a one to one personal service is our main aim. After all we want you to come back, and recommend us to others.

We have all the parts

We supply parts, we have a major stock of new and used parts, or we can get the parts we need within days, fit tyres, undertake major engine work, fault finding, service work and much much more, we can do just about anything your bike needs.
